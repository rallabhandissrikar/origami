FACTS ABOUT ORIGAMI

  1. Origami is derived from two Japanese words: Ori (folded) and Kami (paper).
  2. Origami is the art of paper folding, which is often associated with Japanese culture.
  3. Origami is an ancient art form that is believed to have been around for nearly 2000 years.
